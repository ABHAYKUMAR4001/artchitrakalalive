About Us

Founded with a passion for art, Art Chitrakala Live provides a platform where budding and professional artists can showcase their talent. We host regular art contests across various age groups, distribute significant cash prizes and feature stunning pieces from artists around the world.

Our mission is to nurture creativity, celebrate diversity in art, and connect artists with art lovers. With a dedicated art sale exhibition and no hidden commissions, we help artists reach a wider audience and sell their work. Whether you’re a professional artist or just starting out, there’s a place for you here.
